Our Story

**St. Xavier School, Brahmagiri** is a growing educational institution dedicated to providing quality education and holistic development for students in the region. Located in Brahmagiri, near the culturally rich town of Puri in Odisha, the school focuses on academic excellence, discipline, and character building. With a supportive learning environment, modern teaching methods, and experienced educators, the school encourages students to develop strong knowledge, creativity, and moral values. St. Xavier School aims to nurture responsible citizens who are prepared to face future challenges while staying connected to their cultural roots and community.

Vision

To be the hub of innovation in Odisha.

Mission

Empowering students to think independently.
